About

I am a Ph.D student with Dr Sergiy Bogomolov in Research School of Computer Science (RSCS) at The Australian National University (ANU). My research interest mainly includes hybrid system analysis and hybrid planning.

Beyond these, I am keen to touch projects in a wider range. In the past, I had experience in combinatorial search and application of statistical machine learning including natrual language processing and social media analysis.
